Pakistan's Biggest University

The University of Engineering and Technology, Taxila (UET Taxila) is a public university located in Taxila, Punjab, Pakistan.
It was established in 1975 as a campus of the University of Engineering and Technology,
Lahore and chartered as an independent university in 1993.
